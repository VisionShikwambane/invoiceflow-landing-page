FAQ

Common questions

Do I need a credit card to sign up? +
No — your first 20 invoices are completely free, no card required. You only need to add payment details if you decide to upgrade to the Pro plan.
Can I cancel my Pro plan anytime? +
Yes, absolutely. Cancel at any time and you won't be charged again. Your invoices and client data remain accessible even after cancellation.
Does Invoiceserve handle South African VAT? +
Yes. Invoiceserve has 15% SA VAT built in. You can toggle it on or off per invoice — useful if you're a VAT vendor or not yet registered.
How do the automatic reminders work? +
Once an invoice is overdue, Invoiceserve automatically sends a polite follow-up to your client on your behalf.
Is my data safe and secure? +
Absolutely. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never share your client information with anyone, ever.
